A Little About Me

I'm a husband, father, and programmer. I generally develop web applications with C# and ASP.NET MVC, but I love learning new things. I've messed around with a Netduino Plus and some other Microcontroller programming. In a past professional life I was a PHP programmer. I've also dabbled in Python and Ruby. I haven't found my ideal job yet, but I'm not sure I even know what that is. I think I will ultimately want to be a programming teacher, but for now I want to make some money :)

It's a great feeling when I'm training (teaching) someone and you see the lightbulb turn on. Until I retire as a teacher, I want to learn as much as possible, learning new languages, patterns, styles, techniques, methods, and anything else that will help me ship better software. I once had dreams of being a game programmer, but I don't think that lifestyle is for me (and my family).
